Who we are

A pharmacist's reading of the Korean pharmacy aisle

Not a store. A reference desk for travelers and residents trying to buy the right thing.

EIGHT PHARMACY is a licensed pharmacy based in Seoul. Korean pharmacies stock an enormous range of skincare, everyday remedies, vitamins, and over-the-counter medicines — but for most visitors the labels, ingredients, and usage directions are locked behind a language barrier.

We write practical, symptom-first guides that explain what each product does, who it suits, and how to use it correctly. Every guide is prepared with a pharmacist's eye for active ingredients, sensible dosing, and the precautions that matter — the difference between a retinal that thickens thin under-eye skin and a hydroquinone cream meant only for targeted dark spots.

Because our readers come from all over, each guide is published in English, Japanese, and Chinese. Nothing here is a sales pitch: the goal is to help you choose confidently and use products safely, whether you're visiting Seoul for a week or living here long-term.

What you'll find here

  • Active ingredients and how they actually work
  • Usage steps, timing, and safety precautions
